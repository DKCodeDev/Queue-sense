Features That Make a Difference

Our intelligent queue system adapts to your needs with powerful features.

Weighted Priority Queue

Dynamic priority scoring balances elder care with fairness. No one gets blocked, everyone gets served efficiently.

Flexible Service Windows

20-minute service windows instead of rigid time slots. Early arrivals are handled gracefully.

Elder Assistance Mode

Staff-operated interface for elderly users. Large text, simple forms, and proactive voice announcements.

Real-Time Updates

Live queue status updates every 5 seconds. Know exactly where you stand with instant notifications.

Voice Notifications

Clear voice announcements for token calls. Optimized for elderly users with slow speech.

Smart Analytics

Track wait times, peak hours, and service efficiency. Make data-driven improvements in real-time.

How QueueSense Works

Simple for users, powerful for staff, insightful for admins.

1. Book or Walk-In

Book an appointment with a flexible time window, or simply walk in and join the queue.

2. Get Your Token

Receive a unique token with real-time position tracking and estimated wait time.

3. Get Served

Hear your token announced, proceed to your counter, and receive quality service.
